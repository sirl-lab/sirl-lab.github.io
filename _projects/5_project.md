This project explored how anonymous online platforms can support student mental health and wellbeing in university settings. We designed and deployed an institution-specific anonymous social platform that enabled students to share experiences, seek support, and interact with peers while preserving their privacy. Over a two-month deployment involving  students, we examined how anonymity, peer interaction, and passive engagement influenced emotional wellbeing and community building.
