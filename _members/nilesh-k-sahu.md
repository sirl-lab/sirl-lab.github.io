- Nilesh is working on his Ph.D thesis since January 2022

- His research focuses on digital mental health in India, addressing challenges in developing clinically relevant AI methods for underrepresented populations.
## Contributions

- He received Google PhD Fellowship 2025  
- Adaptive Mental Health Interventions: He leverages reinforcement learning approaches to design and study adaptive mental health interventions, investigating when and how support can be delivered through contextual bandits, user receptivity modeling, and just-in-time adaptive interventions (JITAIs).
- Wearable and Mobile Sensing for Mental Health: He develops multimodal approaches for understanding anxiety and mental wellbeing using wearable, smartphone, physiological, behavioral, facial, acoustic, and language data, while also creating datasets and benchmarks for digital mental health research.
- Natural Language Processing and Human-Centered AI for Mental Health: He develops and evaluates language-model-based approaches for mental health assessment and support, including mental state examination summarization, conversational agents, and studies of conversational AI use in real-world mental health settings.
- He published at the following venues as a lead author or co-author
    - Conferences : COMPASS, COLING, MobileHCI
    - Journals : ACM JCSS, IWMUT, IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing
